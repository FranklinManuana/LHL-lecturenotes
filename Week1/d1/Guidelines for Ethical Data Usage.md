## Guidelines for Ethical Data Usage

Instruction

Read this [5 PRINCIPLES OF DATA ETHICS FOR BUSINESS](https://online.hbs.edu/blog/post/data-ethics) to familiarize yourself with the common themes to consider when it comes to engaging with data responsibly.

>
